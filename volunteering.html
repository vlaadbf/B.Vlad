Volunteering

Neversea 2018/2019

In 2018, I participated in a volunteer activity within the Neversea Festival, in which I had the chance to work in the check-in department . From this activity I acquired communication skills, knowledge in a program on the computer which is based on entering data and activating them and performing tasks correctly in a time interval as short as possible. In 2019, during the same festival, I was assigned as a volunteer to the logistics department , where I have perfectionated the ability to work in a team.
